My Hobbies & Passions

Journaling

I've been journaling for the last couple of years, and it's become such a helpful part of my daily routine. It's amazing how writing things down can help clear my mind and keep me off social media. This was especially helpful when I was recovering from my ACL injury - it gave me a way to process everything I was going through. Journaling has become my go-to method for dealing with stress from school work and staying organized with my thoughts. It's like having a conversation with myself that helps me understand things better and stay focused on what's important.

Photography

Photography has been my creative outlet since I was little! I started out using my parents' old camcorder and phones to take pictures of bunnies and other wildlife. As I grew up, I got to use better cameras, like Canon cameras for school dances, and eventually got my own digital camera. What I love most about photography is that it's just pure fun - it never feels like work. I get to capture special moments and see the world in different ways through my lens. It's awesome to look back at how my photography has grown from those first wildlife shots to the pictures I take today!

Fitness & Working Out

Working out has become such an important part of my daily routine! It's not just about staying in shape - it really helps me feel great both mentally and physically. I love how exercise gives me more energy for tennis and just life in general. Whether I'm doing strength training, stretching, or cardio, staying active helps me perform better on the tennis court and keeps my mind sharp for school. It's amazing how moving your body can make such a big difference in how you feel throughout the day!
